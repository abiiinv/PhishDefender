Email Phishing Simulation

Email Phishing : Email phishing is a cyber attack where hackers send fake emails pretending to be from trusted organizations (e.g., banks, PayPal) to trick users into revealing sensitive information like passwords, credit card details, or personal data.

Key Characteristics: ⁕ Urgency & Fear – Threats like "Your account will be suspended!" ⁕ Fake Links – URLs that look real but redirect to malicious sites. ⁕ Poor Grammar & Spelling – Some phishing emails have mistakes.

From: admin@paypal-security.com
Subject: Urgent: Verify Your PayPal Account Now!

Dear User,

We have noticed some unusual activity on your PayPal account. To ensure your account's security, we require you to verify your account information immediately.

Failure to verify your account within 24 hours may result in account suspension.

Thank you for your prompt attention to this matter.

Sincerely,
PayPal Support
